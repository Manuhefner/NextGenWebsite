Chat Inspector

A Specialized Tool to Detect Accusations and Sensitive Patterns in Chat Histories.

The Challenge: Hidden Risks in Communication

Digital conversations on platforms, in legal discovery, and within corporate communications can contain hidden risks, from false accusations to policy violations. Manually reviewing thousands of messages to identify these sensitive patterns is an impossible task.

Our Solution: Proactive Risk Detection

Chat Inspector is a highly specialized NLP tool designed to analyze large volumes of text-based communication. It goes beyond simple keyword searching to understand context and identify specific, predefined patterns such as accusations, threats, or compliance breaches, flagging them for human review.

Key Features

Contextual Pattern Recognition

Our AI is trained to understand the nuances of language to accurately identify accusations and other complex patterns.

Customizable Classifiers

Tailor the system to detect patterns specific to your industry, platform, or legal case, from harassment to financial fraud.

Secure & Compliant

Designed for sensitive data, providing a secure environment for analysis with detailed reporting for legal and moderation teams.
