The Challenge: The Chaos of Digital Evidence

Legal cases are increasingly reliant on digital evidence scattered across multiple sources: GPS logs, text messages, audio recordings, and social media. Manually piecing this data into a coherent, chronological, and defensible timeline is a monumental and error-prone task for legal teams.

Our Solution: Clarity and Certainty for Legal Teams

Legal Timeline is a specialized AI tool that ingests disparate digital evidence and automatically reconstructs a clear, interactive, and verifiable timeline of events. Our platform saves legal professionals hundreds of hours, reduces human error, and provides powerful visualizations to strengthen their case.

Key Features

Multi-Source Data Ingestion

Securely upload evidence from various sources, including GPS data files, chat exports (WhatsApp, Messenger), and audio recordings.

AI-Powered Timeline Generation

Our AI automatically sorts, cross-references, and places all data points onto a single, interactive chronological timeline.

Interactive Visualization & Export

Explore the timeline visually, filter by evidence type, and export court-ready reports and exhibits with a single click.
