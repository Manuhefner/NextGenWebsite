ChatLink

A Conversational AI Platform for Ethical, Chat-to-Pay Companionship.

The Challenge: The Need for Authentic Connection

In an increasingly isolated digital world, people seek genuine connection. The creator economy provides an avenue, but lacks safe, monetizable, and ethically-grounded tools for non-explicit companionship and support.

Our Solution: Empowering Creators, Connecting People

ChatLink is a mobile platform that empowers creators (models, influencers, advisors) to monetize authentic interactions. Our AI matches users with suitable creators for one-on-one chats, focusing on emotional support and advice, while a secure Stripe integration handles pay-per-interaction billing.

Key Features

Ethical, Non-Explicit Focus

Our platform is strictly designed for supportive, non-explicit conversations, with AI moderation to ensure a safe environment.

Pay-Per-Interaction Monetization

Seamless integration with Stripe allows creators to earn revenue for their time through a secure and transparent payment system.

AI-Powered Matching

Our intelligent algorithm connects users with the creators best suited to their needs and interests, fostering better conversations.
