MapGenius

An Advanced Geo-Visualization Tool for Mapping Movement, Timing, and Operational Data.

The Challenge: Data Lost in Spreadsheets

Logistics, security, and research teams collect vast amounts of location and time-based data. However, this data often remains trapped in static tables, making it nearly impossible to identify patterns, analyze routes, or visualize operational flows effectively.

Our Solution: Data Brought to Life

MapGenius ingests raw GPS and timestamp data and transforms it into rich, interactive maps. Using leading mapping APIs like Mapbox and Google Maps, our platform allows users to visualize movement patterns, analyze logistical efficiency, and uncover critical insights that were previously hidden.

Key Features

GPS & Timestamp Ingestion

Easily upload raw data from various sources to begin your analysis in seconds.

Interactive Map Rendering

Visualize data on high-quality, interactive maps. Pan, zoom, and filter to explore your data dynamically.

Pattern & Anomaly Detection

Our analytical tools help you identify key patterns, logistical bottlenecks, and unusual activity in your datasets.
